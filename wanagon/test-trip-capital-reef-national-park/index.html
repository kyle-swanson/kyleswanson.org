The First Trip - Capital Reef National Park

28 May 2015 on Trips

To test out the Subaru with our trip upgrades, Kendra and I headed down to Capital Reef for the long Memorial day weekend.

The weather was the furthest thing from promising, that usually doesn't stop us, right?

One bonus of the on and off rain was watching miniature flash floods happen. The water falls that formed were amazing!

We did some fun hiking north of the park.

And of course, we brought the Wanagon along!

Even though we got soaked more than a few times, we still had a great time!

Kyle Swanson
